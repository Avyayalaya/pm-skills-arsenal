## Planned Use Cases

### Use Case 1: Cross-Functional Alignment for Platform Migration (Enterprise)
**Scenario:** A Principal PM at a cloud platform company needs to align Engineering, Finance, Legal, and the CTO on a major platform migration that affects 4 teams and requires $2M in unbudgeted spend.
**Key challenge:** Engineering VP is a blocker (her team is at capacity), Finance is skeptical (ROI unclear), CTO is a champion but hasn't publicly committed.
**Frameworks exercised:** F1 Power-Interest-Position, F3 Decision Archaeology, F4 Alignment Sequencing, F5 Objection Pre-emption

### Use Case 2: Executive Buy-in for New Product Line (Growth Stage)
**Scenario:** A PM at a B2B SaaS company needs CEO and board approval to launch a new product line that competes with a strategic partner.
**Key challenge:** Head of Partnerships is a blocker (fears partner retaliation), CEO is neutral (wants data), board member with partner relationship is a swing voter.
**Frameworks exercised:** F2 Coalition Analysis, F3 Decision Archaeology, F5 Objection Pre-emption, F6 Communication Strategy

### Use Case 3: Recovery After Failed Alignment Attempt (Enterprise)
**Scenario:** A PM's initiative to consolidate 3 internal tools into one platform was rejected 6 months ago. New leadership wants to revisit, but institutional memory of the failure shapes every stakeholder's position.
**Key challenge:** Previous champion left the company, two skeptics have been promoted to decision-maker roles, and the original blocker's concerns were never addressed.
**Frameworks exercised:** F3 Decision Archaeology (load-bearing), F1 Power-Interest-Position (re-mapping), F4 Alignment Sequencing (recovery), F7 Alignment Monitoring
